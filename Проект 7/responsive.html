My favorite fragrances summer 2022

Nomade Naturelle Eau de Parfum Chloé

Nomade Naturelle Eau de Parfum by Chloé is a Amber Floral fragrance for women.

This is a new fragrance was launched in 2022.

The nose behind this fragrance is Caroline Dumur.

Top notes are Dates and Mirabelle;

middle notes are Egyptian Jasmine and Freesia;

base notes are Vanilla, Sandalwood and Oakmoss.

Luna Penhaligon's

Luna by Penhaligon's is a fragrance for women and men.

Luna was launched in 2016.

Top notes are Lemon, Bergamot and Bitter Orange;

middle notes are Rose, Juniper Berries and Jasmine;

base notes are Balsam Fir, Musk and Ambergris.

Acqua di Parma Blu Mediterraneo - Fico di Amalfi

Acqua di Parma Blu Mediterraneo - Fico di Amalfi by Acqua di Parma is a Floral Fruity fragrance for women and men.

Acqua di Parma Blu Mediterraneo - Fico di Amalfi was launched in 2006.

Top notes are Grapefruit, Bergamot, Citron and Lemon;

middle notes are Fig Nectar, Jasmine and Pink Pepper;

base notes are Fig Tree, Cedar and Benzoin.
